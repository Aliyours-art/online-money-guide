How to Earn with Just Your Smartphone in Nigeria (2025 Guide)

Smartphones are no longer just for social media and chatting. In 2025, Nigerians are using their phones to earn money daily. Whether you are a student, unemployed, or just looking for extra income, your smartphone can be a powerful tool to change your financial situation. Here are some tested and proven ways to make money with just your phone:

1. Online Freelancing

You can work from anywhere using freelancing platforms like Fiverr, Upwork, and Freelancer. Services such as article writing, graphic design, voice-over, and translation are in high demand. All you need is your smartphone, internet connection, and your skill.

2. Content Creation

If you love being on camera or making skits, start your own YouTube or TikTok channel. Nigerian content creators earn money through ads, sponsorships, and product promotion. Use free editing apps like CapCut or InShot to edit your videos directly on your phone.

3. WhatsApp TV

Create a WhatsApp status TV where you post viral news, funny clips, or products for sale. As your viewers grow, people will pay to advertise through you. It’s an easy way to earn from your daily internet activities.

4. Affiliate Marketing

Promote products and earn commissions for every sale made through your link. Jumia, Konga, Amazon, and even international brands offer affiliate programs. Share your links on social media or WhatsApp groups.

5. Sell Digital Products

You can write an eBook or design a PDF guide using just your phone. Then sell it through Selar, Paystack Storefront, or WhatsApp. Many Nigerians are now selling Canva templates, study materials, and even cooking guides online.

6. Online Surveys and Micro Tasks

Apps like Honeygain, Swagbucks, and Toloka pay users to answer questions, watch ads, or complete small online tasks. It’s not a get-rich-quick method, but it's legit and helps you earn little money daily.

7. Mini Importation Business

Use your phone to buy cheap items from China (like on 1688 or Aliexpress) and sell them on WhatsApp, Jiji, or Instagram. You don’t need a shop—just good photos and marketing.

8. Learn and Teach a Skill

Use platforms like Coursera, YouTube, or Udemy to learn digital skills on your phone. Then teach others what you’ve learned for a fee via WhatsApp, Telegram, or even Zoom. Examples of such skills include graphic design, video editing, and digital marketing.

Yadda Zaka Samu Kuɗi da Wayar Hannunka Kaɗai (2025)

Wayar hannu yanzu ba don kiran waya da Facebook kawai ba ce. A shekarar 2025, matasa da tsofaffi a Najeriya na amfani da wayarsu domin samun kuɗi cikin sauƙi. Ko kai ɗalibi ne, kana zaman gida ba tare da aiki ba, ko kuma kana neman ƙarin kuɗin shiga—wayarka tana iya zama hanyar samun ribar yau da kullum. Ga wasu hanyoyi da zaka iya amfani da wayarka domin samun kuɗi a Najeriya:

1. Yin Aikin Yanar Gizo (Freelancing)

Shiga shafukan Fiverr, Upwork ko Freelancer ka yi aiki daga gida. Ayyukan kamar rubutun labarai, fassarar harsuna, gyaran murya da zane suna da bukata sosai. Kana buƙatar wayar hannu, intanet da kwarewa kaɗai.

2. Yin Bidiyo (Content Creation)

Idan kana da sha’awa wajen yin bidiyo, zaka iya ƙirƙirar tashar YouTube ko TikTok. Ka ɗauki bidiyo da wayarka, ka gyara shi da apps kamar CapCut, sannan ka dora shi. Idan mutane sun kalli bidiyonka da yawa, zaka samu kuɗi daga tallace-tallace da haɗin gwiwa.

3. WhatsApp TV

Ka ƙirƙiri WhatsApp TV inda zaka dinga saka labarai, bidiyo masu ban dariya, da tallace-tallace. Idan mutane sun fara kallon status dinka da yawa, zasu biya ka domin ka tallata musu kaya.

4. Tallan Kayayyaki (Affiliate Marketing)

Shiga shirin affiliate na kamfanoni kamar Jumia da Amazon. Kana amfani da link ɗinka wajen tallata kaya a WhatsApp ko Facebook. Idan wani ya siya, zaka samu riba kai tsaye.

5. Sayar da Kayayyakin Digital

Ka ƙirƙiri eBook ko takardar PDF da wayarka, sannan ka sayar da su ta WhatsApp ko Selar. Mutane da dama suna siyan littattafai, templates, da bayanan koyarwa a yanar gizo yanzu.

6. Cike Tambayoyi da Ayyuka

Apps kamar Honeygain da Toloka suna biyan kuɗi idan ka kammala ayyuka ko ka kalli tallace-tallace. Ba kuɗi mai yawa bane, amma zai taimaka maka ka samu kuɗi kullum.

7. Shigo da Kaya (Mini Importation)

Ka sayo kaya daga China (Aliexpress, 1688), sannan ka sayar da su a WhatsApp, Jiji, ko Instagram. Kana buƙatar hoto mai kyau da fasaha wajen tallata su.

8. Koyo da Koyarwa

Ka koyi sabuwar sana’a a YouTube ko Coursera da wayarka. Bayan haka, ka koyar da wasu a WhatsApp ko Telegram sannan ka karɓi kuɗi. Kamar koyon zane, gyaran bidiyo, ko digital marketing.
