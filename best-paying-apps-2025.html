10 Best Paying Apps in Nigeria (2025)

1. Opay

Opay is a versatile mobile money app that provides users with a wide range of financial services, including cashback rewards, referral bonuses, bill payments, and easy transfers. It’s especially popular among students and small business owners due to its simplicity and user-friendly interface. The app frequently runs promotional campaigns that allow users to earn more cash back on transactions, making it a top choice for anyone looking to make extra money on everyday spending.

2. PalmPay

PalmPay rewards users through multiple avenues such as transactions, referrals, and promotional tasks. You can earn cash rewards by simply using the app daily or encouraging others to sign up using your referral code. The app also offers discounts and cashback on shopping and bill payments, making it a popular app for people who want to combine convenience with earning opportunities. Its growing user base and active reward system make it a standout in the Nigerian fintech space.

3. PiggyVest

PiggyVest is more than just a savings app — it’s a trusted platform for Nigerians who want to save money and earn attractive interest rates over time. The app also offers a referral program where users can earn bonuses for inviting friends and family to start saving. Many users appreciate the easy-to-use interface and the variety of saving plans available, from short-term goals to long-term investment strategies. PiggyVest encourages financial discipline while offering passive income through interest earnings.

4. Branch

Branch is primarily known as a loan app, but it also offers investment options where users can earn interest on the money they invest. The app’s referral program is lucrative, rewarding users with cash or bonuses when their friends successfully borrow or invest. Branch uses technology to provide quick access to loans without collateral, and its growing popularity is due to its transparency, low interest rates, and reliable customer service. It’s ideal for those who want both borrowing and investing options in one platform.

5. Chipper Cash

Chipper Cash is a leading app for sending money across Africa with no transfer fees. It also rewards users who refer friends to the platform. Beyond peer-to-peer payments, users can buy airtime, pay bills, and shop online, all while earning rewards for active engagement. Its easy cross-border transfer capabilities make it popular among Nigerians with family or business interests in neighboring countries. The referral bonuses and loyalty incentives provide added value to frequent users.

6. Cowrywise

Cowrywise is a trusted savings and investment platform designed to help Nigerians grow their wealth through automated investments. Users can set up savings goals, automate deposits, and earn profits from a diverse range of investment portfolios. Cowrywise also incentivizes users with referral bonuses when they invite friends to join. The app’s user-friendly design and transparent fees make it accessible to beginners and experienced investors alike, making it a smart choice for anyone looking to build financial security.

7. JumiaPay

JumiaPay is the payment arm of Nigeria’s biggest online marketplace, offering customers rewards, cashback, and discounts when they shop, recharge, or pay bills using the app. The platform frequently runs seasonal promotions and referral campaigns, allowing users to earn even more. JumiaPay’s seamless integration with Jumia’s shopping platform makes it an essential app for online shoppers who want to save money and get rewarded while spending.

8. GIGM

GIGM, short for God is Good Motors, is a popular bus service app in Nigeria that offers rewards to users who book trips and refer new passengers. Frequent travelers can enjoy discounts, loyalty bonuses, and occasional promo offers that make commuting affordable and rewarding. The app’s referral system encourages word-of-mouth growth, and many users appreciate the convenience of booking tickets and managing trips through the app.

9. FairMoney

FairMoney is a fast loan app that also offers referral rewards and cash incentives for timely loan repayments. Users who pay back their loans on time can enjoy lower interest rates and other financial perks. The app is designed for people who need quick access to credit without complicated paperwork. Its combination of loan services with referral bonuses makes it attractive for those seeking both financial support and ways to earn.

10. Paylater (Now Carbon)

Paylater, now rebranded as Carbon, offers loans, payments, and investment opportunities. Users can earn by referring friends who take loans or invest in Carbon’s savings plans. Carbon provides flexible loan terms and multiple financial services in one app, making it a popular choice for Nigerians looking to manage their finances smartly. The app’s innovative features and customer rewards program make it a standout fintech app in Nigeria.

Manya 10 Apps da Ke Biyan Kuɗi a Najeriya (2025)

1. Opay

Opay wata manhaja ce ta kuɗi ta hannu wadda ke ba da cashback, lada na referral, da hanyoyi na adana kuɗi. Yana da amfani sosai ga ɗalibai da masu ƙananan kasuwanci saboda sauƙin amfani da shi. Opay na yawan shirya wasu gasa da tallace-tallace wanda ke baiwa masu amfani damar samun ƙarin kuɗi a kowane ciniki, wanda hakan ya sa ya zama sanannen app ga masu son samun kuɗi daga abubuwan yau da kullum.

2. PalmPay

PalmPay yana ba masu amfani lada ta hanyoyi da dama kamar yin ciniki, gayyata, da ayyukan talla. Za ka iya samun kuɗi ta amfani da manhaja kullum ko gayyatar wasu su yi rijista ta hanyar lambar gayyata. Manhajar na bayar da ragin kudi da cashback a lokacin siyayya da biyan bills, wanda ya sa ta zama shahara ga masu son haɗa sauƙi da samun kuɗi. Yawan masu amfani da ita da tsarinta na lada na kara yawan masu amfani a Najeriya.

3. PiggyVest

PiggyVest ba wai kawai manhaja ce ta ajiya ba, har ma wata kafa ce da ake amfani da ita wajen adana kuɗi da samun riba mai kyau. Hakanan, manhajar tana da shirin referral inda zaka sami lada idan ka gayyaci abokai su fara ajiya. Masu amfani da yawa suna son sauƙin amfani da ita da nau’ikan shirye-shiryen ajiyar da ake da su, daga na ɗan lokaci zuwa na dogon lokaci. PiggyVest na taimaka wa mutane wajen tsaftace kuɗin su tare da samar da riba mai kyau daga ajiyar su.

4. Branch

Branch app ne da ake amfani da shi wajen neman rance cikin sauri, amma kuma yana ba ka damar saka jari domin samun riba. Shirin referral dinsu yana bada lada ga masu gayyatar abokai da suka karɓi rance ko saka jari. Branch na amfani da fasaha wajen sauƙaƙa samun rance ba tare da jingina ba, kuma yana da kyau saboda rage kashi da kulawa mai kyau ga masu amfani. Ya dace da wadanda ke son samun dama a rance da saka jari a lokaci guda.

5. Chipper Cash

Chipper Cash manhaja ce ta aikawa da kuɗi cikin sauƙi ba tare da caji ba a nahiyar Afirka. Hakanan yana ba masu amfani lada idan suka gayyato abokai. Baya ga aika kuɗi, zaka iya siyan airtime, biyan bills, da yin siyayya a intanet yayin da kake samun lada. Sauƙin tura kuɗi tsakanin ƙasashen Afirka na sa ta zama manhaja mai matuƙar amfani ga waɗanda ke da dangantaka ko kasuwanci a wurare daban-daban. Shirye-shiryen lada da kari suna ƙara wa masu amfani ƙima.

6. Cowrywise

Cowrywise wata manhaja ce ta ajiyar kuɗi da saka jari wadda aka kirkira domin taimakawa ‘yan Najeriya wajen ƙara arziki ta hanyar saka kuɗi ta atomatik. Masu amfani na iya tsara manufofin ajiyarsu, su saita lokacin ajiya, kuma su samu riba daga jarin da suka saka. Cowrywise na ba da lada ga masu gayyatar abokai su shiga wannan manhaja. Sauƙin amfani da bayyananniyar hanyar biyan kuɗi sun sa ya dace da sabbin masu saka jari da ƙwararru.

7. JumiaPay

JumiaPay ɓangaren biyan kuɗi ne na babban kasuwar sayar da kaya ta Jumia a Najeriya. Yana ba masu amfani lada, cashback, da rangwame idan sun yi siyayya, suka biya bills ko suka yi recharge ta wannan manhaja. Hakanan yana shirya manyan rangwamen lokaci-lokaci da shirin referral domin ƙara wa masu amfani kuɗi. Hadakar JumiaPay da Jumia na sa ya zama wajibi ga masu amfani da intanet masu sha’awar samun lada yayin da suke yin siyayya.

8. GIGM

GIGM, wanda ake kira God is Good Motors, shahararriyar manhaja ce ta sufuri a Najeriya wadda ke ba masu tafiya lada idan suka yi booking da kuma ga waɗanda suka kawo sabbin fasinjoji. Masu amfani na kai a kai na samun rangwame, lada na aminci, da tayin musamman. Shirin referral na ƙarfafa masu amfani da kalaman yabo, kuma masu amfani suna yaba sauƙin yin booking da sarrafa tafiyarsu cikin manhajar.

9. FairMoney

FairMoney manhaja ce ta bayar da rance cikin sauri wadda kuma ke ba masu gayyatar abokai lada da kuɗi. Masu biyan rance a kan lokaci na samun rage kashi da ƙarin lada. An tsara manhajar ne domin taimaka wa mutane samun kuɗi cikin sauri ba tare da wahala ba. Haɗa rance da lada na sa ya zama zaɓi mai kyau ga waɗanda ke neman taimakon kuɗi tare da damar samun lada.

10. Paylater (Carbon)

Carbon (wanda aka fi sani da Paylater) yana bayar da rance, biyan kuɗi, da dama na saka jari. Masu amfani na samun riba daga gayyata da saka jari a cikin manhaja. Carbon na ba da zaɓuɓɓuka daban-daban na biyan rance da ayyuka masu yawa a cikin manhaja guda, wanda ya sa ya zama sanannen zaɓi ga ‘yan Najeriya masu son gudanar da kuɗinsu cikin hikima. Fasahohinsa da shirye-shiryen lada sun sa shi zama babban manhaja a kasuwar fintech.
