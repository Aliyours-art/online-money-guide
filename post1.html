How to Make Money Online Without Investment

Learn to earn from your phone or laptop without spending a kobo!

Make Money Without Investment (English)

Making money online without any investment is very possible in today’s digital world. Many platforms offer opportunities to work, learn, and earn without paying any fees. All you need is a smartphone or laptop and internet access. Here are some of the best ways to get started:

1. Freelancing

Websites like Fiverr, Upwork, and Freelancer allow you to offer services like writing, graphic design, voiceover, and more. You only need to sign up, create your profile, and start applying for jobs.

2. Affiliate Marketing

You can promote products from companies and earn a commission for each sale made through your link. Platforms like Expertnaire, Amazon Associates, or Jumia Affiliate are popular in Nigeria.

3. YouTube and TikTok

Create engaging content and build an audience. Once you reach a certain number of followers and views, you can monetize your channel through ads and sponsorships.

4. Online Surveys and Apps

Some apps and websites pay users for completing surveys, watching videos, and downloading apps. Examples include Swagbucks, Toluna, and WowApp.

5. Sell Your Skills on Social Media

If you can write, design, or tutor others, use platforms like Facebook, WhatsApp, and Instagram to advertise your service. Many people get clients just by posting regularly.

Final Tip:

You don’t need to spend money to start. Focus on learning and consistency. Over time, your online hustle will grow and pay you well.

Hanyoyin Samun Kudi Ba Tare da Jari ba (Hausa)

Samun kudi ta yanar gizo ba tare da jari ba yana yiwuwa a wannan zamani na zamani. Akwai shafuka da dama da ke bawa mutane damar yin aiki da samun kudi ba tare da biyan komai ba. Abinda kawai ake bukata shi ne wayar hannu ko kwamfuta da kuma haɗin internet. Ga wasu hanyoyin da za ka iya farawa:

1. Aikin Freelance

Shafuka kamar Fiverr, Upwork da Freelancer suna ba da damar biyan kuɗi ga masu rubutu, zanen hoto, murya da sauransu. Kawai kana buƙatar rajista da kirkirar bayananka.

2. Tallan Haɗin Gwiwa (Affiliate Marketing)

Zaka iya tallata kayayyaki daga kamfanoni, kuma idan wani ya saya ta hanyar link ɗinka, zaka samu kashi daga kuɗin. Shafuka kamar Expertnaire, Amazon Associates, ko Jumia Affiliate suna da amfani.

3. YouTube da TikTok

Yi bidiyoyi masu jan hankali ka tara masu kallo. Da zarar ka samu masu bibiyar da yawa, zaka iya fara samun kuɗi daga talla da haɗin gwiwa da kamfanoni.

4. Cika Tambayoyi da Amfani da Apps

Akwai shafuka da apps da ke biyan kuɗi ga masu cika tambayoyi, kallon bidiyo, ko sauke wasu apps. Misalai sun hada da Swagbucks, Toluna da WowApp.

5. Sayar da Basirarka a Kafafen Sadarwa

Idan kana da basira kamar koyarwa, rubutu, ko zane, ka tallata kanka a Facebook, WhatsApp da Instagram. Yawancin mutane suna samun kwastomomi ta hanyar hakan.
